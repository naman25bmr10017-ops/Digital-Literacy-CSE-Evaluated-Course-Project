Stay Safe Online – Checklist:

- Never share your UPI PIN with anyone
- Do not click on unknown payment links
- Always verify the sender before approving requests
- Use strong passwords for apps
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Do not share OTPs with anyone
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for transactions
- Regularly check your bank statements
- Report fraud immediately on cybercrime.gov.in
- Call helpline number 1930 in case of fraud
